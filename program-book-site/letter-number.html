GRA 321: Technology for Design 1

For this project I randomly picked two letters and a number and had to create a logo from two of the three items I chose. With that logo we had to create a fa-brand that the logo could go with. I got an I R & 4 and decided to create a candle company with the R & 4 called Fourth Realm.
